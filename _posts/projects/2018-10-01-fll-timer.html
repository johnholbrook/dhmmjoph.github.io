FLL-timer.com is a simple, easy-to-use match timer for FIRST® LEGO® League teams and events. It's suitable for use by teams and events of all sizes and experience levels, from small teams parcticing at home to international invitationals, and everything in between.

Features:

  • Large, easy-to-read display
  • Simple Interface
  • Intuitive Keyboard Controls
  • Optional, selectable sounds at match start, T-30 seconds, and match end
  • Customizable timer font, length, and appearance
